1. Executive Summary

The Africa Bitcoin Reserve (ABR) is a sovereign digital reserve protocol designed to enhance monetary coordination, cross-border settlement efficiency, and financial inclusion across African Union member states.

ABR is a Bitcoin-derived Layer-1 blockchain launched with an independent genesis block and modified consensus architecture. It introduces:

A fixed supply of 1,000,000,000 ABR

Hybrid Proof-of-Work / Proof-of-Stake consensus (“AfricaHash”)

Sovereign allocation framework aligned with African Union participation

On-chain treasury governance

Cross-border settlement optimization

Long-term continental economic infrastructure design

ABR is not designed to replace national currencies.
It is designed to function as a strategic digital reserve and settlement layer.
